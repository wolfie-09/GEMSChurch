About Us

We are a church located in the heart of Darbhanga, Bihar.  It is our prayer to see the residents of this city worshipping Jesus Christ through the biblical, Christ-centered messages preached, the songs we sing that glorify Christ and in our fellowship with others. Our mission is rooted in the Gospel power to transform lives by restoring the relationship of men and women with their Creator, and as a result of that, with one another. We are a simple church that neither adds to nor subtracts from the gospel, for it is the gospel itself which is the power of God for salvation to everyone who believes. Our prayer is to build a community of believers that serves Christ and others using the gifts and talents God has given us.

We hope to see you soon!
 Pastor

Mission Statement

To reach the un-reached with the Good News of the Lord Jesus Christ, help them to reach their own and transform the society, primarily in Bihar, Northern States of India and beyond.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to see Jesus Christ build His church, a living and thriving fellowship of believers using all their gifts and talents to impact our community.

Values Statement

As a GEMS family we live by faith for all our ministerial, material, financial and personnel needs. Our God is more than able thus far and will keep according to his promise till the end.
